STEPS TO PROTECT YOUR FLOCK

Housing

  • Ensure proper ventilation and space for your flock.
  • Keep housing clean and dry to prevent diseases.
  • Use secure enclosures to protect from predators.
  • Ensure compliance with local regulations.

Feeding

  • Provide a balanced diet rich in nutrients.
  • Ensure clean and fresh water is always available.
  • Store feed in a dry and secure place to avoid contamination.
  • Monitor feed consumption to detect any health issues early.

Health

  • Regularly check your flock for signs of illness.
  • Consult a veterinarian for vaccinations and treatments.
  • Isolate sick birds to prevent the spread of disease.
  • Maintain a clean and hygienic environment.

Biosecurity

  • Limit access to your farm to essential personnel only.
  • Disinfect equipment and footwear before entering housing areas.
  • Establish a clear protocol for visitors and deliveries.
  • Monitor and control pests that may carry diseases.
